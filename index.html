The main problems in learning

How do we usually learn something? We are preparing for exams and learning tickets. We train ourselves to solve repetitive problems in order to do better on a test, but often in the end this does not give us real knowledge. Cramming quickly disappears and does not benefit.

Conclusion: you also need to be able to learn, but for some reason this is not taught anywhere. What to do with it?

Specific techniques and exercises help to change the approach to learning, make it effective and exciting.

Learning Strategies

5 rules by Barbara Oakley

The two modes of thinking

Deep knowledge arises by alternating between focused and diffuse thinking.

Recall

An ability to recall—to generate the ideas from inside yourself—is one of the key indicators of good learning.

Interleaving

Mixing up your studies with interleaving gives you practice recognizing different kinds of problems that require different approaches.

Questions

As you listen to your teacher, come up with a good question that you really care about.

Illusion of competence

Repeat to yourself, write it down, tell a friend: it just seems to you that you own a new topic.

Barbara Oakley's story

Since childhood, Barbara was struggling with mathematics. She considered herself a complete humanist, and far from being the smartest. In the army, she studied Russian to get a raise and was so successful that she was promoted to commander. But to move forward she had to pass mathematics. And then Barbara came up with her own approach to the sciences. It turned out that if something is "bad" for you given", your hard-earned knowledge is much deeper than those to whom everything is clear at a glance.
